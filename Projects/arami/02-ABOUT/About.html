ABOUT ARAMI GROUP

International Development Trade Company (IDTC) after so many experiences in exporting Iranian products, has found one of the main and essential reasons of undevelopment in exporting products is lack of professional and specialized source of exporting in various languages, which on one hand will be able to present Iranian products and services efficiently, properly and scientifically to all global markets specially region markets. And on the other hand present other products from other countries to Iranian markets.

This company after one year market researching, examining global markets and focusing on the need of finding this source, has approached to design and build this site.

International Development Trade Company has inspired from one of the greatest and most successful B to B sites in the world , "Alibaba" , and also gather one of the most professional Electronic Trade , IT , software , marketing and sale teams.

We hope by designing this specific and specialized website as a useful source for business and trading, help all industrial, agricultural and business men to provide variety information and lead them to achieve their goal in global markets and also provides a great opportunity for other overseas companies to reach iraninan markets for presenting their products.
