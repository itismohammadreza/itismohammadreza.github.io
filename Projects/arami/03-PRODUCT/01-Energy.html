Morshed Gohar

Morshed Gohar Company with previous background and experiences in manufacturing machines for food industries, produced the first “Dough Shaper” machine in 1976. This machine is used in making Bullock bread. Another machine which is manufactured in this factory is “Bread Slicer Machine” which is used for toast and crusty bread. By producing these machines in bread industry, importing of this type was stopped. In the early 1980 Morshed Gohar Company started to design full bakery machinery for puffy and bulky breads and confectioneries. Some of the machines that has been manufacturing till now are “Baking Machines” in 2 model – rotatory and deck – and “Shaping and Baking Process” machine. All the products are designed and manufactured base on international standards. Because of that Morshed Gohar Company started its exporting phase in the early 1990 to Middle Asia and Middle East. Also in 2000 this company began to renew and redesign their product to fulfill various needs for domestic and foreign customers.
